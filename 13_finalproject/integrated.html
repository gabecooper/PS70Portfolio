Final Project: A Simple Tracker


Final Project Integration

My main priority for final project integration was to recreate each aspect of the project to-scale. Having already tested each crucial mechanical step of the machine during the MVP, I was determined to understand what structure and dimensions would best suit the final machine. Here were the changes that I made:

  • Changed "Connect Four Scottish Yoke" dropper to a "Solenoid-style servo motor extension one" because it made the design a lot simpler, and allowed for the motor to be less powerful. There also was too much friction on the sides in the slider.
  • Replaced the small 3D printed body (30mm x 80mm) with a CNC'd foam body that is much longer and can double as marble storage
  • 3D-printed a 300mm arimedes screw and attached it to a DC motor
  • Laser cut supports for the 300mm screw, and hot glued the contraption together
  • Drilled / Sawed pathways on the baseplate to force the marbles post-columns into the elevator and a "waiting path"
